  • Beatrice Kristi Ilejay Laus, known professionally as beabadoobee, is a Filipino-English singer and songwriter.
  • From 2018 to 2021, she released five extended plays under the independent label Dirty Hit: Lice, Patched Up, Loveworm, Space Cadet and Our Extended Play.
  • Beabadoobee served as a supporting act for labelmates the 1975 during several legs of their Music for Cars Tour, as well as American singer Clairo during her Immunity Tour.
